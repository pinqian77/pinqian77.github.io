Biography

My work sits at the intersection of reinforcement learning and ML systems, with a particular interest in RL infra, LLM inference, and production ML serving. I am currently a Machine Learning Engineer at Meta, working on production ranking systems in Core Ads Growth.


Previously, I worked on game AI agents preference optimization at Tencent, ML systems research for LLM inference at Carnegie Mellon University, and earlier reinforcement learning research on portfolio optimization. Across these experiences, I have been most motivated by building efficient and reliable systems that make learning-based agents practical in the real world.
